About

Got Dirt, Need Dirt is a platform that connects truckers with people that need dirt hauled or removed. Truckers (to include non-commercial trucks like your Tundras and Titans) can now make extra income by moving dirt for people. People that need dirt can create a request to remove their dirt. Contractors can also create requests to move dirt to their construction site. Sign up and start moving dirt.

FAQs

How much does it cost?

- Got dirt is free for consumers and contractors to list a job. Truckers will pay a nominal fee of $25 for each lead which will be automatically subtracted from the final invoice.

How many jobs can I apply for?

- It’s a competitive market out there! Truckers can apply for as many jobs as possible. Jobs are usually on a first come first serve basis so reach out as soon as jobs are listed.

What kind of trucks can sign-up?

- DOT Commercial trucks can sign up as well as personally-owned trucks.

Who can use the site?

- Anyone that needs dirt hauled or removed. People can create listings for these jobs for truckers to find and do the work for you.

What kind of dirt can I move?

- We have an extensive list of dirt that our truckers are able to move. Here is a quick list of what we can move; asphalt, boulders, clay, clean fill, concrete, contaminated soil, gravel, peat, recycled material, rip-rap, river rock, rough fill, sand, snow, and topsoil.

When is the mobile app coming out?

- We’re looking at Q2 to have the iOS app out and Q3 to have the android app up. In the meantime, sign up and continue to receive updates when we release these products.
